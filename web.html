Academic Advising

My first hand-coded website, created in March 2024 for an introductory HTML/CSS course. The site explores academic advising at Texas Christian University through six linked pages. I researched national advising statistics, collected graphics from valid sources, and connected data to my personal advising experience. The project strengthened my understanding of document linking, accessibility, and basic web organization..

DCDA Capstone

This capstone project explores how light pollution affects migratory birds and what we can do to reduce its impact. My partner and I designed a multi-page website that combines research, data visualization, and storytelling to make the science easy to understand. The site walks users through the problem, highlights case studies, and ends with simple actions people can take to help.

Travel Blog

Built around a mobile-first responsive design concept. The site serves as a personal travel blog featuring six cities I visited while studying abroad in Rome. These cities include Wengen, Malmö, Innsbruck, Kastelruth, San Marino, and Luxembourg. Each page includes original writing, travel recommendations, and photographs I took during the semester. The project also includes a multi-page navigation bar with a dropdown toggle built in JavaScript for smaller viewports. It represents my first fully responsive, multi-page site coded by hand.
